Timeline of Pink Floyd:

  • 1963 - Formation of Stigma 6 (Roger Waters, Nick Mason, Richard Wright, Keith Noble, Clive Metcalfe, Sheilagh Noble).
  • 1964 - Name changed to Tea Set, Nobles and Metcalfe leave, Bob Klose and Syd Barrett joins the band.
  • 1965 - Name changed to Pink Floyd Sound, Klose leaves.
  • 1966 - "Sound" removed from the name becoming Pink Floyd.
  • 1967 - Pink Floyd signed with EMI:
    • 10 March 1967 - Releasing first single "Arnold Layne" with B-sides "Candy and a Currant Bun".
    • 16 June 1967 - Releasing second single "See Emily Play".
    • 5 August 1967 - Releasing first album The Piper at the Gates of Dawn.
      Due to Berretts LSD usage his mental helt was so bad, that the group added David Gilmour to their line-up.
  • 1968 - Barrett leaves.
    • 29 June 1968 - They released second album A Saucerful of Secrets.
  • 1969
    • 13 June 1969 - The first full-length soundtrack album More released. A soundtack for the film More.
    • 7 November 1969 - First live album Ummagumma.
  • 1970 October 2 - Album Atom Heart Mother released, as Pink Floyd's first number 1 album.
  • 1971 October 31 - Album Meddle released.
  • 1972 June 2 - Second soundtrack album Obscured by Clouds, for the French film La Vallée
  • 1973 March 1 - One of the most commercially successful rock albums of all time The Dark Side of the Moon.
  • 1975 September 12 - Album dedicated to Syd Berrett - Wish You Were Here.
  • 1977 January 23 - 10th studio album Animals released.
  • 1979 November 30 - Pink Floyd's second best selling album The Wall released.
  • 1982 - The Wall concept spawned a live-action/animated musical drama film Pink Floyd – The Wall.
  • 1983 March 21 - Originally planned as a soundtrack album for the film Pink Floyd – The Wall The Final Cut released.
  • 1985 - Roger Waters departure.
  • 1987 September 7 - 13th studio album A Momentary Lapse of Reason released.
  • 1994 March 28 - 14th studio album The Division Bell released.
  • 2005 - Reunion for Live 8 event. First time in 24 years, that aters, Gilmour, Mason and Wright performed together as Pink Floyd.
  • 2006 - Barrett died on 7 July 2006, at his home in Cambridge, aged 60.
  • 2008 - Wright died of an undisclosed form of cancer on 15 September 2008, aged 65.
  • 2014 November 7 - 15th and final studio album The Endless River released.
